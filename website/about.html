ABOUT

Kale Gallery brings together fine line and precision in the disciplines of drawing, cut paper, painting and photography. Jimmy Kale opened Kale Gallery in the fall of 2004 after several years of working with notable photography galleries including Franker Gallery, Howland Greenbird Gallery, and York Richberg Gallery. He is on the faculty of Georgia State College where he teaches and lectures on issues in contemporary photography.
